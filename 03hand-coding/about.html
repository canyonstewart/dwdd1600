About Me

Up Bringing

I was born and raised in Provo, Utah. I am the oldest of 3 and have parents that taught me the importance of loving others, working hard and persuing my passions.

From a young age I fell in love with art. I have been drawing as long as I can remember. Not only that but I have always loved clothes. Around the age of 8 or so I remember annoying my Nana till she would help me attach a BMW patch to a soccer jersey I loved. I won a t-shirt design competition in 6th grade. In highschool I was in charge of designing the t-shrits for the whole school. Designing graphic apparel is in my blood!

Education

I graduated from Timpview Highschool in Provo, UT in May of 2017. I'm currently in the Graphic Design program at Utah Valley University and plan on graduating in less than 2 years with my bachelor degree.
